Place of interest 1

Puijo sports and recreation venue

Puijo sports and recreation venue is a versatile sports centre providing opportunities for sports and exercise around the year. The Puijo stadium located below the Puijo tower is the heart of the venue, and the starting point for an extensive ski track and exercise trail network. The venue also includes a ski jumping hills, downhill ski slope, thePuijo stairs excellent for improving your fitness, and many grass fields. At the outskirts of the venue, you will also find a Finnish baseball stadium and anoutdoor exercise venue. The area also includes a 1.3 km long lit roller skiing track with an asphalt surface (width 4-5 m). Welcome to Puijo!
